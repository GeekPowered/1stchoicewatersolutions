Have you noticed a filmy residue on your glasses, even after washing them? Maybe you’ve seen a whitish tinge to your appliances and home surfaces when you wipe them down. When you leave the shower, does your skin itch? If so, it sounds like you have hard water issues that a water softener can solve. Water softeners, also known as water conditioners, are essential if you want to keep your pipes free of buildup and your dishes and surfaces free of stains. 

But what do water softeners do exactly? Water softeners are devices used in places like homes, restaurants, and car washes that remove dissolved minerals from hard water. Michigan water softener systems remove the hard minerals most common in the area–lime and calcium–to create softer, less dangerous water. Enjoy your water without it leaving its mark on you or your home for 15-20 years to come.
